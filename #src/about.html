OUR PEOPLE BEHIND THE COMPANY

  • Wilson Younan
    Managing Director
    Meet The Founder

    My name is Matthew, and I am the founder of DLVRD Media. My passion is seeing our brands reach new goals that they never saw possible before. It’s what gets me up in the morning and excited for work each and every day. After spending nearly 10 years in the digital marketing agency world, I quickly realized that the traditional agency structure does not work for brands who really are in need of growth. I wanted to work with brands who have a great product or service to not only grow their revenue, but to grow their business holistically. I started working as a consultant with 1-2 brands, and saw that the need was there. With my first 2 brands both coming from poor experiences from working with larger agencies, I knew I had something special going. What is even more incredible, those same two brands are still with me today, and have seen tremendous growth. So, what does a brand do that is doing $70,000/month in revenue? Do they spend 20% of their revenue on an agency that may not even grow them? That would be detrimental to those brands, and I have witnessed it before. That led to where I am today. I created DLVRD Media to fill this void with growing brands. Taking my learnings from my consulting days, I built DLVRD Media around the hands on, quick communication, and high-quality service offering. Our retention rate shows this with 94% of our brands staying with us for years on end. With our unique and customized pricing structure we only succeed when your brand succeeds. That’s how it should be, right?
